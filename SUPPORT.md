## Getting Help
1. Check onboarding and setup in `README.md`.
2. Review account access in Settings.
3. Include screenshots and reproduction steps when contacting support.

## Recommended Issue Format
- Workspace/project name
- Expected behavior
- Actual behavior
- Steps to reproduce
- Browser and OS
